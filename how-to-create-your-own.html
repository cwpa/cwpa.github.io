How to Create Your Own Commedia Dell ‘Arte Character Mask

The process to create your Commedia Dell ‘Arte character mask is fairly simple.  Start by deciding on the character you wish to create.  Here’s a collection of masks as a reference.  Don’t be too concerned about the colors.  Look at the eyes, the nose, the cheeks.  Which one inspires you?

After this 6 step process, you’ll be ready to paint your mask.

Step 1 - Starting with the full mask, draw lines to show where to cut and then remove the excess.  

Step 2 - Using the Model Magic Clay, create the raised areas of the mask.

Step 3 - Lay the Modeling clay on the mask.  Do not press it down.

Step 4 - Lay the modeling clay aside.

Step 5 - Spray the mask with adhesive.  Do this a portion at a time.  It will be less messy than if you spray the entire mask at once.

Step 6 - Lay the modeling clay back on the mask and smooth out the edges.

The modeling clay will need to dry for at least 24 hours before it can be painted.  Once it’s dry you can add the colors.  Once you’ve painted it, you can add ribbons or elastic to hold it on and - BOOM - You have your own unique Commedia Dell ‘Arte Character Mask!
