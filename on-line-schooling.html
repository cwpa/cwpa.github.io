CWPA Distance Learning

As we progress through the 2020-2021 school year, please be assured that the Canal Winchester Schools and our Performing Arts Department are dedicated to providing the highest quality of Education to our students.  While our process has to change, the standards of instruction have not.  

We encourage you to review the guidlines from Ohio Govenor Mike DeWine, the Canal Winchester Schools and the Ohio Department of Education as well as the the guidelines and studies for Performing Arts Education from various professional organizations.
