CWPA On-Line Schooling

All assignments must be Emailed or Google Shared to: tphillips@cwls.us  If you’re sending a video file, please consider using a mp4. file.  This type of file takes up less space and is easier to send.  A file converter is usually free on-line.  If the file is still too big to email, Google Share it with me.

PLEASE contact me if you have any questions or concerns.

If you find yourself a bit bored, take a look at the Optional Assignments.  You can complete these for your own curiosity and report them to me for extra credit.  You can do these Optional Assignments as many times as you wish.  You can even do assingments for classes you’re not enrolled in.  I’ll still give you points.

Try clicking on any of the pages above and follow the various links.  They are all designed to enhance your Music and Theatre Education.
