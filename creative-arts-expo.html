Creative Arts Expo

CWHS On-Line Gallery

Canal Winchester High School believes in the Healing Power of the Arts.  The last 18 months have been a struggle for many in our community.  In early September, 2021, we asked our Students and Staff to use their creative nature to help us heal from the stresses of Covid-19.  These are some of their offerings.
