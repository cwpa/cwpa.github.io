OCC Honor Choir Rehearsal Files - 2017

This year’s OCC Honor Choir will take place Wednesday, November 8 at The Ohio State University.  Participating students will leave CWHS during first and not return during the school day.  We will have rehearsals during the day and present the OCC Honor Choir Concert that evening at 7:00pm in Weigel Hall on the campus of OSU.  We will use a school to take us to OSU, but students will need to arrange their own transportation after the concert.  The concert is free.  Parents and friends are encouraged to attend.  Maps and further infomation will be passed out durning class.

In order to prepare for the concert, all students are required to learn their music before November 8th.  The OCC has provided us with rehearsal files to aid you.  Please download them and begin learning the music ASAP.  

Each song is listed by it’s number from the original score.  Please notice some songs are 4 part, some are more.  Beyond the standard SATB and individual voice parts, you’ll see SATB featuring your voice part.  YouTube performances of the music with the text have also been added.
