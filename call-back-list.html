A Midsummer Night’s Dream — Call-Back List 

I can’t thank everyone enough for taking the risk of auditioning.  It means a lot to me that you’ve taken this step in your journey in the Performing Arts.  I wish there was a way to give everyone the character they wanted. 

Here is the list of everyone’s name which is thought fit to play in our interlude.  Perchance if you are not cast, you will come again in the Spring when we will present “Fame.” - a joyful romp about student’s quest to find their place in the Arts.

I’ve listed each Call-Back name with possible roles I’m considering for you.  Be aware I will be considering other roles as well.  Call-Backs start at 2:45, Thursday.
