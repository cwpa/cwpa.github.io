Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective










Into the early 1990's, Canal Winchester had a community choir, the Canal Winchester Chorale directed by Virginia Heffner.  The Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective is excited to bring this tradition back to our community with the newly-formed Winchester Voices.


Our mission is to create a welcoming space for choral singers of all levels of profidiency and backgrounds.  


This new vocal ensemble is open to everyone without an audition, ages 16 and up.  The only requirement is a love of singing and creating the Art with others.  The Choir's music will range from Scared and Secular, Pop and Broadway, to Folk and Classical.


The exact start-up date has not yet been determined, but we expect to be up and running by the end of February and perform in later Spring.  As the program grows, we will have an annual Holiday Concert, a Spring Concert and our Summer Sings Concert.


The Winchester Voices will be directed by Todd Phillips.  Mr. Phillips (Fil) has nearly 40 years of Choral Music Education experience, the last 35 years as the Canal Winchester High School Vocal Music Educator.  Under his direction, the High School Vocal Department consistently scored high ratings in OMEA Solo and Ensemble, District and State Competitions.
