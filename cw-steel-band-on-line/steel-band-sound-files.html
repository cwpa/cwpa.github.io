Steel Band Sound Files

I put these files together a few years ago for Tony when he was out giging and needed some tunes with backing tracks.  You can download the Lead Pan music if you don’t have a copy of the melody.  The tunes without a pdf and the full band mp3.  You have these tunes in your folder. They are up to tempo. If that's are too fast, look on-line for software that can slow down an mp3. I use the 'Amazing Slow Downer.’  There are lot of them out there.  Only a couple of these tunes have the changes in the Lead part.  If you’re soloing, they are all Diatonic to the Key Signature.  

If you want a particular tune added to the site, let me know.

How about a few tunes with Non-Diatonic Changes

These songs are jazz style backing tracks without any melody.  The pdf is a lead sheet for the chord changes and you’ll need it to properly follow the chord changes.  You're free to make up any melody that your heart desires.
