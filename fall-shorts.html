Fall Shorts

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------














Canal Winchester HS Performing Arts Department was proud to perform ‘All Together Now!' Music Theatre International’s Global Event Cebrating Local Theatre on November 12 and 13 in the Oley Speaks Auditorium.  Over 5000 performances were scheduled for that weekend.  From Hong Kong to Madison, Wisconsin, Canal Winchester Performing Arts joined nearly 3000 organizations as we celebrated the return of live theatre.
