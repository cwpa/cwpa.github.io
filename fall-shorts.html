Fall Shorts

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------













     The Canal Winchester Drama Department was excited to present our Fall Shorts Festival for 2022-23, 'An Evening on the BBC.' This was an evening of English sketch Comedy, Drama and a few suprises.  Our shows were Friday, Nov 18 at 7:00pm and Saturday, Nov 19 at 7:00pm.  Both performances took place in the Canal Winchester Local Schools' historic Oley Speaks Auditorium, 100 Washington Street, Canal Winchester.
