March 10, 2020 Testing Day Alternative Assignments

The posted assignments are required for all 9th and 10th grade students and will be graded.  11th and 12th grade students are invited to do them as well for extra credit and to improve their skills.

PA Class will email or hand in a hard copy of their assignment.  All other classes should email a screen shot of their results to tphillips@cwls.us.  Theory, Choir and Guitar students should repeat the assignment until they earn at least an 80%.
