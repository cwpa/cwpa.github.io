Oley Speaks Auditorium

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

     Most of the Performing Arts Department’s events take place in the Canal Winchester Schools' Historic Oley Speaks Auditorium.  The auditorium, located at 100 Washington St, is named after Canal Winchester native and mid-20 Century, Popular American composer, Oley Speaks.  


     The 640 seat, classic proscenium auditorium is equipped  with a 24 channel sound system and a 62 channel lighting system.  The 17’ by 46’ stage is equipped with moving-head lights, LED pars, scrim and cyc curtains.  With a large rehearsal room, green room and ample parking, the Oley Speaks Auditorium is ideal for any event in the Canal Winchester area.  


To inquire about rental space, please call 614-837-4533.
