Music Theory Graded Homework

Students must have at least an 80% on each Homework assignment to recieve credit.  Each assignment is 40 questions.  After you have completed 40 questions, stop, save your work and then repeat the assignemt if you did not achieve at least 32 out of the 40 or you wish to improve your grade beyond the 80%.
