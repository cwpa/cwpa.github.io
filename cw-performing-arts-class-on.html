Performing Arts Class - Required Assignments

3rd Quarter Grades - These assignemnts are closed and can not be redone.

1) Assigned - 3/23, Due - 3/27:  (50pts)  Due to the nature of our Silent Movie time-frame, the better movies rely on the use of Stock Characters.  Follow these links and research the nature of Stock Characters and their importance to story telling:  Youtube, Project Gutenberg.  Next you need to Download this page to create a list of a dozen types of your favorite kinds of Stock Characters, why you like them and what makes them unique.  

_____________________________________________________________________________

4th Quarter Grades - If you are not pleased with your grade, work on it and resubmit the assignment.  The goal is Mastery, not One and Done.

2) Assigned - 3/25, Due 4/3:  (50pts)  Silent Movies are based on scenarios, descriptions of actions, locations and events; not dialogue.  Create a Silent Movie scenario for 4 to 6 Stock Characters with 9 to 12 scenes.  Describe the actions, locations and events in each scene.  Be sure to use a traditional story arch with a beginning, rising action, climax and conclusion. Remember not every character needs to be in each scene.  Use this form.

3) Assigned - 4/1, Due 4/7:   (30pts)  Using the Silent Movie scenario from Assignemnt 2, design a movie poster for your silent movie.  You may use any graphics software and classroom apporpriate images you’d like.  Your ChromeBook has options and you can find many others on line.  Do not submit hand drawn/colored pencils drawings.  If you do a seard of famous movie posters, you’ll see that a great poster uses the three elements of design - Color - Texture - Detail.  A great poster will show just enough about the story to hook the audience and stir their imagination.  Be sure to add the needed text - Title, Actors, A Teaser Phrase, ….. 

4)  Assgned - 4/8, Due 4/24:  (50pts Individual, 50pts Group)  Given the continued shutdown, I believe we taken the Silent Movie project about as far as we can.  We are now going to shift over to creating a Radio Play that you can do in your own home.  Click here to see the discription of the project.   A Draft of your completed script must be posted in your Google Doc by Sat 4/18. If you have never heard a classic radio play, you need to do a search on Radio Plays and listen to at least 20 minutes of one to get the idea. 

5)  Assigned - 4/14, Due 4/27:  (20pts)  Go to YouTube or similar on-line video sites and do a search on ‘Broadway Behind the Scenes.’  You’ll find many stories on famous shows.  Report to me the following:  What show was covered.  Where it is/was performed.  Who are lead actors.  Who played the leads.  What was surprising.  

Optional Extra Credit Assignments

1)  (30pts)  You can find nearly anything on-line.  Go to those similar sites and do a search for the top Broadway shows (Musical and Non-Musical) of different eras, 30’s, 40’s, ….  Now look to see if you can find a full performance version of one of those shows.  You can also check subscription services like Hulu, Netfix, Amazon …   Watch the entire video. Report to me the following:  The Show Title.  Who performed it, (HS, College, Professional, …)  What was the main plot line.  The names of the leads characters.  How does it differ from Theatre in 2020. 

2)  (25pts) My wife and daughter have Synesthesia, do you?  Huh?  What’s Synesthesia?  Is it dangerous?  No, Synesthsia is neurological phenomenon where your body gets one kind of sensory input (touch, sound, taste, sight or smell) and it triggers an involuntary response from one of your other senses.  You hear something but it keys a visual response. Studies vary, but about 10% of the population has sgnificant Synesthisic responses. Nearly everyone has some mild Synesthsia.  Have you ever heard a sound and your skin crawled?  Have you looked at a picure of your favorite food and without trying to, you can taste it?  To find out more, watch this short video.  You’ll find a lot of information on the web about Synesthesia.  You’ll even find quality on-line tests that will help you see if you have some Synesthisic traits.  Here’s one.  ( You will need to create a log-in.) If you take the test, send me a copy of your results and I’ll add 25 points to your extra credit grade.
