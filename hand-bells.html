Winchester Chimes

Our Hand Bell and Chime Ensemble is the latest addition to the Canal Winchester Performing Arts Department.  The ensemble is open to all students at Canal Winchester High School. The department is committed to creating excellence in Music Education using the Academic Content Standards laid down by the Ohio Department of Education.
