Creative Arts Expo - Visual Arts

Ceramics, Drawing, Digital Design, Painting, Photography, Sculpture

Artwork must be an original piece created during high school (grades 9-12) and completed solely by the participant. All artworks must be school appropriate and not include vulgar, offensive or demeaning imagery.  If you have any questions or concerns, contact Mrs. Pearson at: tpearson@cwls.us

Your project can be framed or unframed and should not exceed 24” x 24”.

2D Category 

After the creation of your project, take a photo in jpg format, of the work and attach it to the Creative Arts Expo submission form.  Please describe on the submission form the type of media you used to create the work. 

Drawing - Pencil, Pen or Charcoal. 

Painting - Watercolors, Acrylic or Tempera. 

Photography - Film or Digital (Black/White or Color). 

Digital Design/Computer Graphics

3D Category  

After the creation of your project, take atleast 2 photos, in a jpg format, of your work from different angles. These photos will need to be combined into one document, (Word, Google, etc) for upload.  Attach this document to the Creative Arts Expo submission form.  Please describe on the submission form the type of media you used to create the work. 

Ceramics - Functional or Decorative Pottery. 

Sculpture - Mixed Media, Wire, Metal, Wood, etc.
