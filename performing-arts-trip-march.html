Performing Arts Trip March 2017

Start Spreading The News!

 

     Every three years, our department takes a Spring Break trip to New York City.  Our next trip will be this school year, March 27-31, 2017.  We encourage you to look at the itinerary and see the great opportunities “The Big Apple” offers.  You’ll see it’s filled with the best Arts the city can provide; a Classical Concert, a Broadway Show, a Museum, an Off-Broadway show and many of the most famous tourist attractions.

    We have taken more than a dozen student trips in my 32 years in the classroom and worked with Bob Rogers Travel many times.  They are well established and have a high reputation for covering all the potential circumstances.  We know you’ll be happy with their work.

     The trip is being offered to any student in the High School Performing Arts Department: Band, Choir and Drama programs.  We are also offering it to parents and adults who are interested in being Chaperones.  (Don’t let that concern you.  We’ve learned that this type of trip does not draw students with behavior issues.)  All students will be housed 4 to a room.  The adults have options of 4, 3, 2 or 1 to a room.

     Included with the itinerary are the instructions on how to register on line and reserve your spot.  We will be taking a chartered motor coach of 55 seats.  Once it’s filled, there’ll be no more reservations accepted.  The window for registration is open September 1, 2016 at midnight.

     If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Todd Phillips at tphillips@cwls.us
