3rd Quarter Listening Test

This page is for only those students who were absent on Friday, March 13 and missed the 3rd Quarter Listening Test.  By completeing the the process described below, I can give you full credit for the missing 100 point test.  It must be submitted to me by no later than the end of the day, Friday, March 27.

1)  Download the asnswer document in either a Word or PDF format.  You can either mark your answers electronicly or print the page, mark your answers, scan it or take a picture of it.  Return the file to me before the deadline. 

2)  Create a ‘testing environment.’  You may listen to each sound file a maximum of 3 times before completing the assessment.  Each sound file holds 8 questions.  You may pause the file at any time as you work through the questions.  You may not use any electronic or acoustic divice to check you answers before submitting the answer document.

3)  Listen to the attached sound files in the presence of an adult that will email me that you kept a ’testing enviroment’ during the assessment.  A varification email from the adult’s email address is absolutely required for any grade to be given.  Without that email, I am required to give you a ‘0’ for the assessment.
