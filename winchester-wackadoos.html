Winchester Wackadoos

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



It's time for some community Theatre!  With the success of the Winchester Voices community choir, the Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective has created the Winchester Wackadoos, our Community Street Theatre Troupe.



Do you like improv? Or prefer a scripted part? Do you like creating big characters? Do you bring a special skill? Mime? Juggling? Do you work best behind the scenes?


Do you want to join us in this new experience?



These first performances of our Street Theatre Troupe are designed to highlight the skills of our performers.  The scripts will be in the 10 minute range so rehearsals will be scheduled around the actors' availability.  Depending on the number performers, we may form multiple ensembles to perform in different locations on different schedules throughout the afternoon.



As the troupe is up and running, we are looking for opportunities to perform at Downtown Canal Winchester events throughout the year.  This will include the Historic Ghost Tours and Christmas in the Village.   Rehearsals are scheduled to meet the needs of upcoming performances and the actor's availbility.  




Follow the link below to sign up for this exciting new adventure!


Winchester Street Theatre Troupe


Canal Winchester Historic Tours on October 18th and 19th (Friday and Saturday)


There will be 7 scripted stops on the tour this year, including 2 new stops! One will be a new feature on a house and the other will be featuring a new person. We are using 5 locations that have not been featured on the tour in many years. And even trying a few new things like one walking stop!


All of the official tour stops will be fully scripted but we also have the opportunity for some improv. In between the scripted stops there will be "run on" characters. These are much more open and can become what we want. We typically use a location to create a fictional character. For example, we would have someone outside the opera house trying to find patrons for their show. Depending on the actors comfort level with improv this can be as open or structured as they need. We'll be happy to come up with prompts for the actor or leave it all to them!  Whatever you need!
