Winchester Consort

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Our Early Music Consort, performing on period instruments,  is unique for a High School Music Department.  The ensemble is open to all students at Canal Winchester High School. The Department is committed to creating excellence in Music Education using the Academic Content Standards laid down by the Ohio Department of Education.  The Winchester Consort can be heard as part of the department's Annual Shakespereian Madrigal Dinner Theatre.


For a complete listing of Department performances and rehearsal schedules, click: Performing Arts Calendar


All parents are encouraged to attend our CW Performing Arts Boosters meetings the Second Monday of each month at 7:00pm in CWHS Cafetorium.
