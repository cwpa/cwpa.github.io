MS Practice Tracks Fall '24

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


On this page you'll find the resources to prepare for our annual Holiday Jam Concert, Sunday, Dec 8, 7:00pm at David's UCC.  Since we only meet once a week and Thanksgiving  Break takes a rehearsal from us, it's important that you spend time at home preparing for the concert.  


You can download the sheet music for our tunes and call up the Virtual Pan and practice pan on-line. This works best on an iPad or Tablet.  Spend a bit of time just feeling comfortable with the layout of notes on the Lead Pan and using both hands.  


Be aware the Virtual Pan website uses the alternative name for the

Lead Pan = Tenor - Low C.


Your next step is to call up the paractice track for the tunes and then paly along with it.  The sound file has the Laed part included so you can hear how it all fits together as you practice at home.  


Let me kown if you have any questions or concerns.  WinchesterSteelCo@gmail.com
