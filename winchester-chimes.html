Winchester Chimes

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Our Handbell and Chime Ensemble is the latest addition to the Canal Winchester Performing Arts Department.  The 4 Octave Handbell - 5 Octave Handchime ensemble is open to all students at Canal Winchester High School. The Department is committed to creating excellence in Music Education using the Academic Content Standards laid down by the Ohio Department of Education.  The Winchester Chimes performs around the Community for special events and throughout the academic year.



For more information about the Winchester Chimes, please contact: WinchesterChimes@gmail.com
