Winchester Chimes

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Our Hand Bell and Chime Ensemble is the latest addition to the Canal Winchester Performing Arts Department.  The 4 Octave Hand Bell - 5 Octave Hand Chime ensemble is open to all students at Canal Winchester High School. The Department is committed to creating excellence in Music Education using the Academic Content Standards laid down by the Ohio Department of Education.  The Winchester Chimes performs around the Community for special events and at each Quarter concert along with the CWHS Vocal Department.


For a complete listing of Department performances and rehearsal schedules, click: Performing Arts Calendar


For more informationa bout the Winchester Chimes, please contact: WinchesterChimes@gmail.com



All parents are encouraged to attend our CW Performing Arts Boosters meetings the Second Monday of each month at 7:00pm in CWHS Cafetorium.
