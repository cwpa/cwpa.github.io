Welcome

Canal Winchester High School offers possibly the most diverse Performing Arts program in central Ohio. Between the three branches of the Department (Drama, Instrumental and Vocal), CWHS students have nearly 2 dozen different classes and performing ensembles.

For more information about our programs, please email: tphillips@cwls.us
