Graded Solfege Lines

Each experience level will be required to sing 5 Solfege lines.  I’ve broken the levels down by number of semesters you’ve had in the CWHS Choral program.  You should include this semester as part of your experience level.  Sing each line the way it is listed, starting on our regular Do.  Record yourself and email/Google share it back to me. The Do is also the reference pitch from Assignment #7.  Click Here for the reference pitch.  You are not required to perform with reference pitch playing, but practicing with the pitch will help you stay in tune.  

I have created a sound file to help you rehearse your assignment.  Click on the link after the Solfege line.  Your goal is to sing these lines a cappella, without any accompaniment.  This assessment is woth a total of 100pts, 20 pts per Solfege line. If you feel insecure about your performance, you can record yourself singing along with the sound file, but I will deduct 2 points for each time you sing with the file.  Your grade will be determined by your fluency using the Solfege and pitch accuracy.  Let me kow if you have a concern or question.
