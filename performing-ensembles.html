Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective

A Community Of Life-Long Artists

Follow us on FaceBook  - CW Performing Arts Collective

The Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective is excited to announce our inaugural year performing ensembles staring in the Winter of 2024.  


Many of the details are still being worked through, but we can say there will be a Winchester Voices Community Choir starting Thursday, Feburary 22.  Our mission is to create a welcoming space for choral singers of all levels of profidiency and backgrounds.  The only requirement?  That you love to sing and make music with others.  


Our latest performing ensemble is the  Winchester Street Theatre community Theatre troupe starting up in April 2024.  


We'll be happy to answer your questions and take suggestions on how we can best serve our community in the Performing Arts.  The Canal Winchester Performing Arts Collective can be reach through CWPACollective@gmail.com
