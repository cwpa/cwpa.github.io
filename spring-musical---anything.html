Spring Musical - Anything Goes!

It is time for the drama department’s spring musical! We will rehearse Monday through Thursday from 3:30pm-7:30pm (depending on the day) but students will only be called when their scene is rehearsing. Our performances will be on April 25th (7pm) and 26th (2pm and 7pm).

The S.S. American is sailing between New York and England with some colorful passengers: Reno Sweeney, a popular nightclub singer, her pal Billy Cocker, A Wall Street broker who has come aboard to win the favor of his beloved Hope Harcourt (who is engaged to passenger, Sir Evelyn Oakleigh), and conman Moonface Martin, “Public Enemy #13”. Song, dance, and antics ensue as Reno and Moonface help Billy win the love of his life.

Auditions will at Oley Speaks Auditorium in the Education Center.

Open auditions are February 24th from 4pm - 7:30pm and  February 26th from 4pm - 7:30pm.   Coming both days is highly encouraged but students are only required to attend one day. CALL BACKS – February 27th from 4pm - 6pm

Auditions will require three things –

Singing - Come prepared to sing only 16 bars (30-40 seconds) of a song with sheet music or a back up track (a cappella pieces will not be accepted)

Acting - read any of the scenes in the audition script *Not required if you only want to be in the chorus

Dancing - learn 2 short dance routines – One will be tap

Audition scripts, which are a small sampling from show, are available in the West Wing Music Room or can be downloaded here.  For more information about the show, click here.  Contact the director at:  aphillips@cwls.us
