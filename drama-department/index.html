Drama Department

Canal Winchester High School offers Performing Arts and Theatre classes open to all CWHS students.  The Department is committed to create excellence in Theatre Education using the Academic Content Standards laid down by the Ohio Department of Education.

Tickets are now on sale for this year’s Fall Shorts Festival, Nov 18 and 19, 7pm, Speaks Audiorium.  Contact 614-833-2157 for more information.
